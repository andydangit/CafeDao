Banh Mi

A Vietnamese sandwich fusion of meats and vegetables from native Vietnamese cuisine such as pork sausage, cucumber, pickled carrots, and pickled daikon combined with condiments from French cuisine.

Vietnmess Coffee

Vietnamese coffee is often the Robusta type associated with the strong and distinctive flavor, which differs from the prevalent one in the Western nations called Arabica coffee.

Desert

Traditional Vietnamese sweet beverage, dessert soup or pudding. Varieties of Chè are made with mung beans, black-eyed peas, kidney beans, tapioca, jelly, fruit, and coconut cream

Cafee Dao

is the product of the merging of ideas and family support. Chieu Le, the founder, came to America and settled in San Jose, CA in 1980. He began taking adult education classes to study English and frequently bought food from a catering truck before and after class. Seeing the potential of a portable food service vehicle, Chieu Le worked on a catering truck to better understand the business. By 1981, he bought his first catering truck and started his route around San Jose.
